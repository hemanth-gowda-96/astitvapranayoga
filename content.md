## Core Concepts

### Astitva (The Meaning)
The state of being having a purpose of owning one’s existence, a conscious and powerful presence rather than just being or passing through Present Existence.

### Prana (The Force)
Breath, the life-giving force. The harmony between breath and movement can be achieved only by cultivating balance between these two elements.

## The Four Pillars

### 1. Authentic Yoga Asana
Traditional lineage-based practice adapted for modern bodies and sustainability.

### 2. Mindful Breathwork
Techniques to regulate the nervous system and cultivate vital life force (Prana).

### 3. Guided Meditation
Cultivating mental stillness and overcoming the fluctuations of the mind.

### 4. Holistic Living
Principles that bridge traditional wisdom with modern anatomical understanding.
