About the Shala

Grounded in tradition, shaped for today.

Astitva Prana Yoga Shala is a sanctuary in Mysuru dedicated to the art of conscious movement and breath.

Led by Hemanth, our practice honors the traditional Mysuru lineage while adapting to the needs of modern bodies. We focus on sustainability, precision, and the quiet power of consistency.

  • Intentional small-group classes for personal guidance
  • Progressive teaching rooted in traditional lineage
  • Support for therapeutic goals and injury recovery
  • A space for curiosity, humility, and inner steadiness
