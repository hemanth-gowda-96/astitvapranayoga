About the shala

Grounded in Mysuru tradition, shaped by modern practitioners

Astitva Prana Yoga Shala is a dedicated practice space in Mysuru, Karnataka, offering thoughtfully structured classes that honour traditional sequencing while respecting the needs of today’s bodies and lifestyles.

Led by Hemanth, a teacher with more than a decade of experience, each session weaves together alignment-based asana, pranayama, and simple meditation to cultivate clarity, resilience, and steadiness. Students are welcomed at every stage of practice, from first-timers to long-term practitioners refining their craft.

What guides our work

  • Personalized attention in intentionally small groups
  • Clear, progressive teaching rooted in Mysuru lineage
  • Careful support around injury, fatigue, and burnout
  • Space that encourages curiosity, humility, and joy

“My intention is to help you build a practice that feels sustainable—one that supports your life, not the other way around.”

— Hemanth, Founder & Lead Teacher
